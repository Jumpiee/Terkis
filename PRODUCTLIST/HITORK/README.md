# HITORK Intelligent Valve Actuators - Smart Automation for the Modern Industry

## Short Description
Step into the future of valve control with HITORK. Combining IoT connectivity with rugged mechanical engineering, HITORK actuators provide high-precision positioning and remote monitoring for smart factories.

## Full Description
HITORK represents the cutting edge in valve automation, offering both intelligent electric actuators and rugged pneumatic solutions. The **HITORK 2.0 Intelligent Electric Actuators** (by Hankun) integrate IoT, Bluetooth, and non-intrusive calibration for high-precision control in smart factories. For heavy-duty industrial environments, the **Hytork XL Pneumatic Actuators** (by Emerson) provide legendary reliability with a modular design that ensures safety and ease of maintenance. Whether you require the digital sophistication of electric control or the raw power of pneumatic actuation, HITORK delivers unmatched performance.

## Product Features
*   **IoT & Bluetooth Connectivity:** Remote monitoring and setup via mobile app without ever opening the actuator housing.
*   **Absolute Positioning:** High-precision encoders ensure position data is never lost, even during total power failures.
*   **Non-Intrusive Calibration:** Preserves the enclosure seal by using infrared or Bluetooth for all setup and calibration tasks.
*   **Modular Safety:** The patented SAFEKEY system in pneumatic models makes end-cap removal significantly safer for maintenance personnel.
*   **Extreme Corrosion Protection:** Specialized coatings like "Ceramigard" and FEP provide superior protection against harsh chemical environments.
