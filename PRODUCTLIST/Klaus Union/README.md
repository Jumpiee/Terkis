# Klaus Union Sealless Magnetic Drive Pumps - Zero Leakage Hermetic Safety

## Short Description
Ensure absolute safety for hazardous fluids with Klaus Union. Our sealless magnetic drive pumps eliminate emissions and mechanical seal failures, providing a hermetically sealed solution for your most critical processes.

## Full Description
Klaus Union is a global leader and pioneer in sealless pump technology, having introduced the world's first magnetic drive pump in 1955. These pumps are designed for the hermetically sealed handling of hazardous, aggressive, or high-value fluids where zero leakage is mandatory. By replacing traditional mechanical seals with a magnetic coupling and a static containment shell, Klaus Union ensures absolute safety for personnel and the environment, even in the most extreme temperature and pressure conditions.

## Product Features
*   **100% Leak-Free Performance:** Sealless design eliminates the primary source of pump emissions and costly seal support systems.
*   **Hermetic Isolation:** A static containment shell completely isolates the process medium from the atmosphere.
*   **Extreme Temperature Range:** Specialized models handle temperatures from -120°C up to +450°C (+550°C optional).
*   **High-Pressure Capability:** Rated for system pressures up to PN 400 (5,800 psi) for demanding industrial tasks.
*   **API 685 Compliant:** Available in heavy-duty configurations specifically designed for the oil, gas, and petrochemical industries.
