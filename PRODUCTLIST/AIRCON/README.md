# AIRCON High-Performance Valve Actuators - Precision Automation for Industrial Valves

## Short Description
Automate your process with confidence using AIRCON actuators. Designed for high cycle life and extreme reliability, these actuators provide the precision control needed for complex industrial environments.

## Full Description
AIRCON, a division of Flo-Tite Valves and Controls Inc., is a leader in industrial valve automation. They specialize in high-performance pneumatic and electric actuators designed to automate quarter-turn valves like ball, butterfly, and plug valves. Known for their reliability and precision, AIRCON products are widely used in chemical processing, water treatment, and general industrial automation. With certifications like ISO 5211 and NEMA 4X, AIRCON ensures your automated valve assemblies perform flawlessly in even the harshest conditions.

## Product Features
*   **Ultra-Long Cycle Life:** Pneumatic models rated for up to 1,000,000 cycles for maximum uptime.
*   **Space-Saving Design:** Kompact Series delivers double the torque in a reduced footprint.
*   **Intelligent Electric Control:** Pro-Torq actuators feature modulating options and efficient gear systems.
*   **Plug-and-Play Integration:** Conforms to ISO 5211, DIN 3337, and NAMUR standards.
*   **Weatherproof Durability:** IP67 and NEMA 4/4X ratings protect against corrosion and outdoor elements.
