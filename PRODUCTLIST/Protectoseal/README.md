# Protectoseal Vapor Control & Flame Arresters - World-Class Tank Safety

## Short Description
Protect your facility with the industry standard in tank safety. Protectoseal's E-Flow® technology provides superior flame arrest and vapor management with minimal pressure drop.

## Full Description
Protectoseal is the industry standard for high-quality vapor control and tank protection equipment. They specialize in safety products that prevent the propagation of flames into storage tanks and manage the safe release of internal pressure and vacuum. Utilizing advanced technologies like **E-Flow®**, Protectoseal products offer superior flow performance with minimal pressure drop, ensuring the structural integrity of tanks and the safety of industrial facilities worldwide. From deflagration to detonation protection, Protectoseal has the expertise to keep your operations secure.

## Product Features
*   **Patented E-Flow® Technology:** Optimized element design offers better flow and significantly lower pressure drop than traditional arresters.
*   **Full-Spectrum Protection:** Solutions for both deflagration and detonation flame fronts in all gas groups (B, C, D).
*   **Modular Arrester Elements:** Easily removable cores allow for fast cleaning or replacement without disturbing the piping.
*   **High-Precision Venting:** Pressure and vacuum relief vents engineered to prevent tank collapse or over-pressurization during operations.
*   **Global Certifications:** Type-tested to EN ISO 16852, ATEX certified, with many UL Listed and FM Approved models available.
