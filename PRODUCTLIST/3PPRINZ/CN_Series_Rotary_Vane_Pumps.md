## Short Description
The **3P Prinz CN Series Rotary Vane Pumps** are high-performance, positive displacement pumps designed for transferring fluids across a wide range of viscosities. Featuring a wear-compensating design with self-adjusting vanes, these pumps provide outstanding volumetric efficiency, high suction lift, and self-priming capabilities, making them ideal for demanding industrial, marine, and petrochemical applications.

- **Self-Priming Capability:** Automatic priming with exceptionally high suction lift.
- **Wear Compensation:** Vanes automatically slide out of the rotor slots to adjust for wear, maintaining peak performance.
- **Low Power Consumption:** Engineered for higher flow rates at lower operating speeds and energy input.
- **API 676 Compliance:** Available in versions meeting the stringent requirements of the American Petroleum Institute.

## Detailed Description

### Overview
The **CN Series Rotary Vane Pumps** represent the state of the art in positive displacement pump technology. They excel in handling low-viscosity, volatile fluids (such as gasoline, solvents, and liquefied gases) as well as medium-to-high viscosity liquids. By operating at relatively low speeds compared to centrifugal pumps, the CN Series minimizes fluid shear and mechanical wear.

### Operating Principle
The pump consists of a rotor with slots mounted eccentrically inside a cylindrical pump casing. As the rotor turns, the self-adjusting vanes slide radially outward under centrifugal force and hydraulic pressure, maintaining continuous contact with the casing wall. This creates expanding chambers on the suction side, drawing fluid into the pump, and contracting chambers on the discharge side, forcing the fluid out. 

### Design Advantages
- **Dry-Run Capability:** The self-lubricating vane materials allow for short periods of dry running during priming.
- **In-Line Maintenance:** The pump's design allows for inspection and replacement of vanes and mechanical seals without disconnecting the pump body from the piping system.
- **Flexible Drive Configurations:** Can be coupled to electric motors, diesel engines, hydraulic motors, or truck-mounted power take-offs (PTO).
