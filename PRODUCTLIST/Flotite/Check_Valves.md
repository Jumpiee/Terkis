## Short Description
The **Flo-Tite IVT Check Valves** are high-integrity backflow prevention solutions designed to protect process equipment and piping systems from flow reversal. Available in cast steel swing check, forged steel piston/lift check, dual-plate wafer check, and specialized PFA-lined ball check configurations, these valves ensure low pressure drop and tight sealing across municipal water, chemical, oil/gas, and power utility services.

- **Automatic Backflow Prevention:** Automatically reacts to flow changes; requires no external actuation or power.
- **Low Pressure Drop:** Streamlined internal pathways optimize flow capacity and minimize turbulence.
- **Multiple Configurations:** Swing, piston, lift, dual-plate, and ball check options to suit horizontal or vertical piping.
- **Severe Service Ready:** Available with spring-assisted closures for fast response to prevent water hammer.

## Detailed Description

### Overview
Flo-Tite offers check valves under both the **IVT (International Valve Technologies)** brand and the core **Flo-Tite** brand (for specialty lined process applications). These non-return valves are designed to allow fluid flow in only one direction, preventing reverse-flow contamination or pressure surges that could damage upstream pumps and systems.

### Major Product Categories & Series

#### 1. Cast Steel Swing Check Valves (IVT CS-SC)
For larger pipelines where low flow velocity and minimal pressure drop are required:
*   **Standards & Design:** Designed to BS 1868 / ASME B16.34. Features a bolted cover, internal hinge pin design (eliminating external shell penetration leaks), and a rotating disc to ensure uniform wear.
*   **Size & Class:** Sizes 2" to 24" (ANSI Class 150, 300, and 600).

#### 2. Forged Steel Piston & Lift Check Valves (IVT FS-PC / FS-LC)
For small-bore, high-pressure utility lines and steam systems:
*   **Standards & Design:** Designed to API 602. Piston checks utilize a spring-loaded piston that lifts with forward flow. Lift checks use a free-floating disc.
*   **Size & Class:** Sizes 1/2" to 2" (ANSI Class 800 and Class 1500).
*   **Connections:** Threaded (NPT) or Socket Weld (SW).

#### 3. Dual Plate Wafer Check Valves (IVT DP-CV)
Space-saving, lightweight backflow preventers for water and industrial lines:
*   **Standards & Design:** Designed to API 594. Features a wafer-style body with two spring-loaded half-moon plates that pivot around a central shaft.
*   **Advantages:** Compact face-to-face dimensions reduce weight by up to 80% compared to swing checks, eliminating the need for expensive piping supports and preventing water hammer due to rapid spring closure.
*   **Size & Class:** Sizes 2" to 24" (ANSI Class 150 and Class 300).

#### 4. PFA Lined Ball Check Valves (Flo-Tite Chem-Flo Series)
Specifically engineered for high-purity and severely corrosive chemical process lines:
*   **Design:** A floating solid PTFE ball acts as the closure member, sealing against a thick PFA body lining.
*   **Materials:** PFA-lined body ensures maximum chemical inertness, making it suitable for acids, chlorine, and highly aggressive chemical media.

## Applications & Use Cases
*   **Pump Discharge Isolation:** Preventing reverse flow and protecting centrifugal pumps from water hammer.
*   **Chemical Lines:** Handling sulfuric, hydrochloric, or nitric acids using PFA-lined ball check valves.
*   **Boiler Feedwater Lines:** High-pressure backflow prevention on boilers using forged steel Class 800/1500 piston check valves.
*   **Water Distribution & HVAC:** Compact wafer dual-plate checks used in space-constrained cooling towers and pumping stations.
