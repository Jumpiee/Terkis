## Short Description
The **Flo-Tite IVT Gate Valves** are designed for reliable bi-directional isolation of fluid flows in high-pressure and high-temperature piping systems. Offered in cast steel (Class 150/300/600) and forged steel (Class 800/1500) configurations, these valves are engineered for low flow resistance and bubble-tight shutoff in power generation, refining, petrochemical, and general process plants.

- **Low Pressure Drop:** Full-port flow path minimizes turbulence and line pressure loss.
- **Outside Screw & Yoke (OS&Y):** Rising stem design keeps stem threads protected from process media, facilitating easy lubrication.
- **Flexible Wedge Design:** Cast steel series utilize a flexible wedge to prevent binding due to thermal expansion.
- **Heavy Bolted Bonnet:** Rugged body-to-bonnet gasket joint ensures high-integrity pressure containment.

## Detailed Description

### Overview
Flo-Tite's gate valve solutions are represented under the **IVT (International Valve Technologies)** brand. Built to meet stringent industry standards (API 600 and API 602), these gate valves are designed strictly for fully open or fully closed isolation services (not for throttling) where minimal flow restriction is required.

### Major Product Categories & Series

#### 1. Cast Steel Gate Valves (IVT CS-GT)
Engineered for general utility, steam distribution, and hydrocarbon processing lines:
*   **Standards & Design:** Designed to API 600 / ASME B16.34. Features a bolted bonnet, OS&Y construction, rising stem, and non-rising handwheel.
*   **Flexible Wedge:** Wedge design accommodates pipe stresses and temperature fluctuations without sticking.
*   **Size & Class:** Available in sizes 2" through 36" (ANSI Class 150, 300, and 600).

#### 2. Forged Steel Gate Valves (IVT FS-GT)
Designed for small-bore, high-pressure utility lines, boiler piping, and instrumentation systems:
*   **Standards & Design:** Designed to API 602. Solid wedge design with a bolted bonnet or welded bonnet for high-pressure isolation.
*   **Size & Class:** Available in sizes 1/2" through 2" (ANSI Class 800 and Class 1500).
*   **Connections:** Available with threaded (NPT) or socket-weld (SW) ends.

## Applications & Use Cases
*   **Power Plants:** High-pressure steam boiler pipelines and isolation systems using forged steel Class 1500 valves.
*   **Petroleum Refining:** Hydrocarbon isolation and pipeline manifolds utilizing cast steel Class 150/300/600 valves.
*   **Chemical Processing:** Utility header systems, water loops, and air lines requiring low pressure drops.
*   **Oil & Gas Gathering:** Midstream terminal installations and manifolds.
