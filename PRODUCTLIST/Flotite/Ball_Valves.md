## Short Description
The **Flo-Tite Ball Valves** represent a comprehensive range of industrial flow control solutions, ranging from utility-grade threaded and flanged valves to high-pressure, severe-service, multi-port, and specialty lined configurations. Designed for reliable shutoff, modular configuration, and automation compatibility, they serve industries ranging from chemical processing and oil/gas to food, beverage, and pharmaceuticals.

- **Automation Ready:** Integrally cast ISO 5211 actuator mounting pads facilitate seamless actuation.
- **Multiple Configurations:** Available in 1-piece, 2-piece, 3-piece, flanged, wafer, and multi-port styles.
- **Severe Service Built:** Options for high pressure (up to 6000 psi), cryogenic service, metal seats, and PFA linings.
- **Standards Compliant:** Meets API 607 (Fire Safe), API 6D (Trunnion), ASME B16.34, and NACE MR0175.

## Detailed Description

### Overview
Flo-Tite Ball Valves are globally recognized for engineering excellence. With a complete line-up covering standard thread-end valves, 3-piece maintenance-friendly valves, space-saving wafer designs, heavy-duty trunnions, and corrosion-resistant PFA-lined valves, Flo-Tite provides high-reliability options for any process.

### Major Product Categories & Series

#### 1. Threaded, Economy, & Standard 1-Piece / 2-Piece Valves
Designed for utility, commercial, and standard industrial piping systems:
*   **Fusion S23 / S23SS:** 1-Piece Stainless Steel Full Port Threaded Ball Valve rated for 250 PSI SWP.
*   **Fusion T23:** 2-Piece Threaded Ball Valve.
*   **Flotron S3000 / Flotron-II S6000:** High-pressure threaded industrial models.
*   **Kingtron ST336 / T70 / Omni T12:** Standard utility and threaded service valves.
*   **EconoFlo T-45 / T82 / T80SS:** Economy-tier utility ball valves.
*   **T91 / T92 RB & T91-B / T92-B JOJ:** Reduced bore and generic utility configurations.

#### 2. Direct Mount (Automation-Ready) Series
Features an integral ISO 5211 direct mounting pad, eliminating the need for brackets and adapters:
*   **DM 2000 / DMV-2000:** 2-Piece 2000 PSI WOG body with integral ISO 5211 direct mounting pad. The DMV version utilizes a V-ball for throttling control.
*   **DM 110 / DMV-110:** Direct-mount economy line featuring standard or V-Ball components.
*   **DM 310 / EconoFlo DM300:** 3-Piece direct mount economy series.
*   **DM 51 / DM 52 / DM 85 / DM80SS:** Industrial-grade direct-mount automation units.
*   **DM-MPT100 / DM-MPT200:** Multi-port direct mount variations.
*   **DM-W150:** Direct-mount wafer-style space-saving ball valve.
*   **EconoFlo DMF15:** Direct-mount flanged end economy valve.

#### 3. 3-Piece Industrial & Severe Service Valves
Offers a "swing-out" design for easy in-line inspection, seat cleaning, and seal replacement:
*   **Tri-Star 410 / 420 (Carbon Steel) & 510 / 520 (Stainless Steel):** High-performance 3-piece ball valves for demanding process industries.
*   **Multi-Choice 300 / 200:** Highly customizable 3-piece standard series.
*   **Multi-Choice Weld-In-Place (335, 325, 225, 235):** 3-piece series engineered specifically to be welded directly into the pipeline without disassembly, protecting internal seals.
*   **Tri-Pro HPF 40, 50 / HPS 40, 50 / HPF 3000:** High-pressure, severe-service 3-piece process valves.
*   **Titan 1540 / 1550:** Heavy-duty, high-pressure 3-piece series.
*   **Multi-Choice Sanitary 350 / 370:** Polish-finished 3-piece valves matching food, beverage, and pharmaceutical standards.
*   **Pro-Max 361 / 362:** High-performance, heavy-duty severe service series.
*   **Econo-Groove 190 / 390:** 3-Piece ball valves featuring grooved mechanical pipe connection ends.

#### 4. Flanged & Segmented Process Valves
For larger process pipelines requiring robust bolted flange or wafer installation:
*   **Full-Flo F150 / F300:** Standard 2-Piece Full Port Flanged Valves (ANSI Class 150 & 300).
*   **EC Series (EC F15 / EC F30):** Econo-Clean/Econo-Chem flanged configurations.
*   **Uni-Flo (SF150 / SF300):** Standard port / 1-Piece reduced port flanged options.
*   **Sentinel Series (RV-150 / RV-300 / RV-600):** High-performance segmented or eccentric V-port control valves for slurry or throttling applications.
*   **Petro F60:** Specialized flanged service valve optimized for oil, petroleum, and hydrocarbon handling.
*   **EZ Series (RF15 / RF30):** Easily maintained flanged processing line.
*   **Kompact Ball Valve W150 / FDUE 3000:** Wafer-style space-saving flanged designs.

#### 5. Multi-Port (3-Way & 4-Way) Valves
Ideal for fluid diversion, mixing, and multi-directional flow control:
*   **Trans-Flo MPF 15 / MPF 30 / MPF 60:** Multi-port 3 & 4-way flanged ball valves spanning ANSI classes 150 through 600.
*   **MPF 154 / MPT 104:** Dedicated 4-way flanged and threaded options.
*   **MPT 100:** Threaded 3-way multi-port configuration.
*   **Bottom Entry MPF 155:** 3-Way bottom entry flanged multi-port profile.
*   **Bottom Flow 3W HPF / HPS:** High-pressure specialized 3-way bottom-entry diversion.
*   **MPT 355 / MPT 365 / MPT 366 / MPT 355 JN:** Multi-port instrumentation-level small-bore ball valves.

#### 6. Specialty & Heavy Engineered Valves
For extreme conditions, corrosive media, and specialized mechanical piping:
*   **TM Series Trunnion (TM150 / TM300 / TM600):** Large-bore pipeline trunnion ball valves built to API-6D standards for midstream and heavy industrial applications.
*   **Control Set DM V60-SS:** Precision modulation stainless control package.
*   **EconoChem 110 / 120 / 130 / 190:** Chemical process-focused specialized alloys.
*   **Chem-Flo Series / PFA Lined Ball Valves:** PFA-lined ball valves designed for handling extremely corrosive media.
*   **PFA Lined Ball Check Valves:** Combined check/ball configurations.
*   **Cryo Series:** Extended bonnet valves built for liquid nitrogen and extreme cryogenic environments.
*   **Ceramic Valve Series:** Exceptionally hard ceramic internals for extreme abrasive slurry wear resistance.
*   **Flush Bottom Tank Series (TK300):** Unique design engineered to mount flush with tank bottoms for zero-dead-space drainage.
*   **Media Containment (Series 140):** Advanced safety valves featuring primary seal monitoring mechanisms to prevent ambient leaks.

## Applications & Use Cases
*   **Chemical Processing:** Handling highly corrosive acids, bases, and organic compounds using Chem-Flo PFA-lined or Ceramic series.
*   **Oil & Gas Midstream:** Standard piping, gathering systems, and transmission pipelines with TM Series Trunnion valves.
*   **Process Automation:** Fast-acting automated loops leveraging DM-series direct mount valves and pneumatics.
*   **Pharmaceutical & Food:** Clean steam, API manufacturing, and sanitary pipelines using Multi-Choice Sanitary series.
*   **High-Pressure / High-Temp Industrial:** High-cycle steam lines and abrasive slurry handling with Tri-Pro or Pro-Max series.
