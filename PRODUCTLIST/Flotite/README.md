# Flo-Tite Valves - High-Performance Flow Control Solutions

## Short Description
Maximize control and process reliability with Flo-Tite valves. Specializing in high-performance ball valves, resilient-seated and high-performance butterfly valves, and a robust selection of gate, globe, and check valves, Flo-Tite delivers severe-service capability for the chemical, energy, and process industries.

## Full Description
Flo-Tite, Inc. is a premier global manufacturer of high-quality ball valves, butterfly valves, and actuation equipment, complemented by its IVT (International Valve Technologies) line of gate, globe, and check valves. Designed to meet strict standards such as ANSI, ASME, API, and NACE, Flo-Tite valves are engineered for severe-service, high-pressure, and highly corrosive process environments. By offering innovative features like ISO 5211 direct-mount automation pads, metal-to-metal seating for extreme temperatures, and PFA linings, Flo-Tite provides robust, long-lasting flow control solutions across the oil & gas, chemical processing, power generation, and wastewater industries.

## Product Features
*   **Automation-Ready Design:** Integral ISO 5211 mounting pads simplify mounting of pneumatic and electric actuators.
*   **Severe Service Capability:** Metal-seated options and specialized alloy structures withstand temperatures up to 1200°F (650°C) and high-abrasion slurries.
*   **Bubble-Tight Shutoff:** Precision-ground balls and high-performance seats ensure zero leakage.
*   **Corrosion Resistance:** Chem-Flo PFA-lined valves handle extremely aggressive chemical media.
*   **Industry Compliance:** Built and tested in accordance with API 6D, API 607 (Fire Safe), ASME B16.34, and NACE MR0175.
