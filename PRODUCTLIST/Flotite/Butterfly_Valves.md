## Short Description
The **Flo-Tite Max-Seal Butterfly Valves** offer versatile, high-integrity flow isolation and control. Available in resilient seated (Models 51/52), double-offset high-performance, and severe-service triple-offset designs, these valves are optimized for reliable operation across utility water, HVAC, chemical processing, petrochemical, and power generation environments.

- **Automation Ready:** ISO 5211 mounting pads allow direct coupling of actuator packages without brackets.
- **Low Seat Wear:** Double offset and triple offset geometries eliminate friction during operation, extending seal life.
- **Bi-Directional Shutoff:** Offers bubble-tight sealing in both flow directions.
- **Extended Size Range:** Available from 2" up to 36" (and larger custom sizes upon request).

## Detailed Description

### Overview
Flo-Tite's butterfly valve division, operating under the **Max-Seal** brand, provides specialized options for diverse piping needs. From standard commercial utility service to high-cycle process automation and critical zero-leakage steam applications, Max-Seal valves ensure high performance and low total cost of ownership.

### Major Product Categories & Series

#### 1. Resilient Seated Performance Series
Ideal for water distribution, waste treatment, HVAC, and general industrial applications:
*   **Model 51 (Wafer Style) & Model 52 (Lug Style):** Premium resilient seated valves featuring a blowout-proof 2-piece stem design, a phenolic-backed cartridge seat, and a self-adjusting stem seal.
*   **Features:** Integrally cast ISO 5211 direct mounting pad. The cartridge-style seat ensures easy replacement in the field.

#### 2. High Performance (Double Offset) Series
Engineered for chemical processing, refinery, paper mills, and industrial power plants:
*   **Double Eccentric Design:** The stem is offset from the center of the valve body and disc, creating a cam-like motion during opening/closing. This design minimizes seat wear and torque.
*   **Seat Configurations:** Soft seats (PTFE, RPTFE, TFM) for chemical resistance, or metal seats for high temperatures.
*   **ANSI Classes:** Available in ANSI Class 150 and Class 300 configurations.

#### 3. Triple Offset Valves (TOV Series)
The ultimate solution for high-temperature steam, hydrocarbons, and severe-service applications:
*   **Triple Eccentric Design:** Combines double offset geometry with a conical sealing surface angle. This eliminates all rubbing/sliding between the disc seal ring and body seat during travel, achieving a true torque-seated, zero-friction closure.
*   **Zero Leakage Performance:** Solid metal-to-metal sealing utilizing a laminated metal/graphite disc seal ring against a hard-faced stellite body seat.

## Applications & Use Cases
*   **HVAC & Water Utilities:** Standard flow isolation, cooling towers, and potable water treatment via resilient seated Model 51/52.
*   **Chemical Processing:** Throttling control and corrosive fluid transport utilizing high-performance double-offset PTFE-seated valves.
*   **Power Plants & Steam Systems:** High-pressure steam isolation and boiler feed lines using Triple Offset (TOV) metal-seated valves.
*   **Marine & Shipbuilding:** Seawater intake lines using Nickel Aluminum Bronze bodies and discs.
