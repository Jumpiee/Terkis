## Short Description
The **Flo-Tite IVT Globe Valves** are engineered for precise flow throttling, regulation, and leak-tight isolation in high-pressure and high-temperature service. Available in cast steel (Class 150/300/600) and forged steel (Class 800/1500) configurations—including bellows-seal and piston-valve options—these valves provide high-integrity performance in utility, petrochemical, and refining operations.

- **Precise Regulation:** Discs are plug-type or needle-type to ensure progressive and stable throttling characteristics.
- **Outside Screw & Yoke (OS&Y):** Rising stem configuration shields stem threads from system media for long life.
- **Zero Stem Emissions:** Available bellows-seal models eliminate potential packing leaks in hazardous media.
- **Robust Disc Guide:** Heavy-duty body guide systems ensure accurate alignment of the disc to the seat, minimizing wear.

## Detailed Description

### Overview
Flo-Tite's globe valve solutions, marketed under the **IVT (International Valve Technologies)** brand, represent the industry standard for throttling service. Unlike gate valves, globe valves are designed to operate in partially open positions to regulate flow rate, pressure, or temperature in process pipelines.

### Major Product Categories & Series

#### 1. Cast Steel Globe Valves (IVT CS-GL)
Designed for larger process pipelines carrying steam, gases, or liquids:
*   **Standards & Design:** Designed to BS 1873 / ASME B16.34. Features bolted bonnet, OS&Y rising stem, and a renewable seat ring.
*   **Seat Geometry:** Plug-type disc provides a wide contact area for robust throttling control and positive shutoff.
*   **Size & Class:** Available in sizes 2" through 12" (ANSI Class 150, 300, and 600).

#### 2. Forged Steel Globe Valves (IVT FS-GL)
Designed for small-bore, high-pressure bypass loops, sampling lines, and auxiliary pipelines:
*   **Standards & Design:** Designed to API 602. Features a bolted bonnet or welded bonnet structure with high-durability packing.
*   **Size & Class:** Sizes 1/2" to 2" (ANSI Class 800 and Class 1500).
*   **Connections:** Available with threaded (NPT) or socket-weld (SW) ends.

#### 3. Specialty Bellow-Seal Globe Valves (IVT BS-GL)
Engineered for chemical process lines handling toxic, flammable, or aggressive media:
*   **Leak-Free Stem:** A metal bellows is welded to the stem and body bonnet, providing a hermetic metallic barrier that eliminates stem packing leaks.
*   **Safety Backup:** Includes standard packing chambers as a secondary backup seal.

#### 4. Forged Steel Piston Valves
*   **Design:** Utilizes a piston-style closure element instead of a standard disc, providing reliable shutoff in hot oil and thermal fluid services.

## Applications & Use Cases
*   **Steam Utility Control:** Precise regulation of steam supply lines and bypass systems in power plants.
*   **Chemical Process Control:** Isolation and throttling of highly volatile organic compounds or acids using bellows-seal models.
*   **Thermal Fluid & Hot Oil Systems:** Hot oil circulation systems requiring high-integrity sealing.
*   **Boiler Feedwater Systems:** Throttling feedwater flow to steam drums.
