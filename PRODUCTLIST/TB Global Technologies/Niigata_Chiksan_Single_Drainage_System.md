## Short Description
The **TB-NIIGATA Chiksan Single Drainage System** is a high-reliability, jointed steel pipe rainwater drainage system designed for external Floating Roof Tanks (FRTs). Co-developed by Toyo Kanetsu and Tokyo Boeki Engineering, this system uses specialized BD-series swivel joints to allow smooth drainage articulation as the tank roof rises and falls. Designed as a cost-effective, space-saving alternative to conventional drainage systems, it eliminates the risks of kinking, twisting, and mechanical damage associated with flexible hoses.

- **Size Range:** 2-1/2" to 12" (2-1/2B to 12B).
- **Max Working Pressure (Internal):** 3.43 MPa (34.3 bar).
- **Max Working Pressure (External):** 0.25 MPa (2.5 bar).
- **Design Life:** Service life expectancy of up to 30 years with minimal maintenance.
- **Joint Material:** High-strength S40C Carbon Steel with corrosion-resistant overlays.

## Detailed Description

### Overview
External floating roof tanks collect rainwater on their roofs, which must be drained continuously to prevent the roof from sinking. Traditional flexible hose drainage systems are prone to twisting, looping, and catching on internal tank equipment (such as roof support legs). The **TB-NIIGATA Chiksan Single Drainage System** utilizes rigid steel piping interconnected with specialized articulating swivel joints. This ensures that the drainage line folds and unfolds in a single, predictable vertical plane, keeping it completely clear of internal tank obstructions.

### Swivel Joint Engineering (BD / BDR Series)
The core of the system is the **Chiksan BD Swivel Joint**, engineered specifically for submerged service in petroleum storage tanks:
*   **Dual-Pressure Sealing:** Designed to handle both internal pressure (drained rainwater) and external pressure (surrounding product).
*   **Main Packing (Internal Pressure):** Features NBR or FPM (Viton) elastomer seals reinforced with an outer stainless steel ring. This outer ring prevents seal deformation and extrusion under high pressure.
*   **Double X-Ring (External Pressure):** Incorporates an outer FPM and inner NBR double X-ring seal configuration to prevent product ingress into the drain line.
*   **Corrosion Protection:** Critical sealing and bearing surfaces are clad with stainless steel weld overlays, ensuring the joint continues to rotate smoothly even after years of submersion in crude oil or corrosive chemicals.

### Design Advantages
1.  **Realization of Low Price:** Engineered to cost almost half compared to conventional multi-line or complex jointed systems.
2.  **Simple and Compact Layout:** Uses a straight-line folding arrangement, making it easy to position around support legs, gauge wells, and other internal tank apparatus.
3.  **Adequate Strength & Stability:** All-steel construction resists hydraulic shocks and provides structural stability during rapid filling or draining operations.
4.  **Excellent Durability:** Built for a maintenance-free service life of up to 30 years under normal operating conditions.
