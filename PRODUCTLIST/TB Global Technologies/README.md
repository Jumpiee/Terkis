# Niigata Single Drainage System - The Ultimate Floating Roof Tank Protection

## Short Description
Eliminate drainage risks with the Niigata Single Drainage System. Outlasting flexible hoses by decades, this rigid articulated piping system provides a safe, predictable path for rainwater drainage in floating roof tanks.

## Full Description
TB Global Technologies (formerly Niigata Loading Systems) is the world leader in specialized articulated piping systems for the petroleum industry. The **Niigata Single Drainage System** is a high-performance, rigid articulated pipe system designed specifically for **External Floating Roof Tanks (EFRT)**. It provides a safe and predictable path for rainwater drainage, eliminating the common risks of kinking, looping, and interference associated with traditional flexible hose drains. Engineered for a 30-year service life, it is the most reliable and cost-effective choice for modern tank farm management.

## Product Features
*   **30-Year Service Life:** Engineered for extreme durability, significantly outlasting rubber hose systems and reducing long-term replacement costs.
*   **Stable & Predictable Path:** Rigid pipe structure prevents the drain from drifting, looping, or abrading against the tank floor or internal supports.
*   **Frictionless Articulation:** High-performance swivel joints with integrated grease nipples ensure smooth movement even after decades of service.
*   **Advanced Pressure Sealing:** Unique Double X-ring seal with a stainless steel outer ring resists deformation under both internal and external pressure.
*   **Space-Efficient Design:** Compact, straight-line arrangement minimizes interference with internal tank legs and roof support structures.
