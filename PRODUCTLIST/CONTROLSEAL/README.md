# ControlSeal Rising Stem Ball Valves - Zero Leakage Friction-Free Performance

## Short Description
Eliminate seat wear and ensure bubble-tight shutoff with ControlSeal Rising Stem Ball Valves. Featuring the HELIXSTEM® mechanism, these valves are the ultimate solution for critical, high-cycle, and abrasive services.

## Full Description
ControlSeal specializes in high-performance **Rising Stem Ball Valves (RSBV)** designed for critical applications where zero leakage and long-term reliability are non-negotiable. Utilizing the innovative **HELIXSTEM®** mechanism, these valves combine linear and rotary motion to tilt the ball away from the seat before rotating. This friction-free operation eliminates seat wear, making it the ideal solution for abrasive media, extreme temperatures, and high-cycle environments. Trusted in Molecular Sieve, Hydrogen, and Hot Oil services worldwide, ControlSeal represents the pinnacle of valve engineering for the oil and gas and petrochemical industries.

## Product Features
*   **Friction-Free Sealing:** Non-contact opening and closing prevent seat damage and extend valve life significantly.
*   **Absolute Bubble-Tight Shutoff:** Mechanically energized metal-to-metal sealing ensures zero leakage even in severe conditions.
*   **Maintenance Simplified:** Top-entry design allows for easy in-line maintenance and inspection without removal from the pipeline.
*   **Inherently Safe:** Single-seat design eliminates pressure buildup in the body cavity, making it safe for volatile media.
*   **Self-Cleaning Action:** High-velocity flow during operation naturally removes debris from sealing surfaces.
