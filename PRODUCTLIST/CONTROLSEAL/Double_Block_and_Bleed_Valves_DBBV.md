## Short Description
The **Control Seal Double Block and Bleed Valve (DBBV)** is a non-lubricated expanding plug valve engineered to provide provable, 100% bubble-tight shut-off. Serving as a single-valve replacement for double valve-and-bleed configurations, it features a friction-free design where the seal slips make contact only at the final closing moment. This completely eliminates seat wear and provides superior isolation for pipelines, metering stations, and tank terminals.

- **Size Range:** 1" to 48" (DN 25 to DN 1200)
- **Pressure Classes:** ASME 150# to 2500#
- **Temperature Range:** -196°C to +250°C
- **Sealing Performance:** Mechanically energized dual-sealing (Double Isolation and Bleed - DIB) with zero leakage.

## Detailed Description

### Overview
In critical applications such as aviation fueling, petroleum bulk storage, and pipeline metering, standard gate or ball valves often suffer from minor seat leakage that can contaminate product or compromise safety. The **Control Seal DBBV** solves this problem by using two independent slips mounted on a tapered plug. When closed, these slips expand mechanically against the valve body to block pressure from both upstream and downstream directions, allowing the cavity to be bled to verify zero leakage.

### Operating Principle
- **Opening:** As the valve begins to open, the plug is lifted vertically. This retracts the slips away from the body seating area. The plug then rotates 90° to the fully open position without any contact between the seals and the valve body.
- **Closing:** The plug rotates 90° without rubbing. Once it reaches the closed position, the stem drives the tapered plug downward, expanding the slips against the body seats. Sealing is achieved mechanically without relying on line pressure.

### Design Advantages
- **Friction-Free Quarter-Turn:** Prevents seal degradation and reduces operating torque, extending seal life up to 10-15 years without maintenance.
- **Provable Zero Leakage:** Cavity bleed valve allows operators to test and confirm seal integrity under full line pressure.
- **Top Entry Design:** Slip seals can be replaced from the top of the valve without removing the body from the pipeline, minimizing downtime.

## Key Features & Benefits
*   **Dual Expanding Slips:** Mechanically energized Viton, Nitrile, or Teflon slips provide reliable double-isolation.
*   **Non-Lubricated:** Eliminates the risk of process contamination from valve grease and lowers overall maintenance costs.
*   **Reduced Footprint:** Replaces traditional two-valve-and-spool bypass setups, saving significant space and cost.
*   **Fire-Safe Design:** Complies with API 6FA and ISO 10497 standards; soft seals are protected by secondary metal-to-metal back-up seals.

## Applications & Use Cases
*   **Custody Transfer Metering:** Isolation at inlet/outlet of positive displacement or turbine meters to prevent measurement errors.
*   **Tank Farms & Terminals:** Product isolation to prevent cross-contamination during storage and distribution.
*   **Airport Fueling Systems:** Hydrant isolation valves where bubble-tight shut-off is critical for safety and leak detection.
*   **Multi-Product Pipelines:** Dividing lines to prevent mixing of different fuel grades.
