# Faure Herman High-Precision Flow Meters - Custody Transfer Accuracy

## Short Description
Achieve unmatched measurement precision with Faure Herman. Specializing in helical turbine and ultrasonic technologies, these meters ensure extreme accuracy for oil, gas, and aerospace applications.

## Full Description
Faure Herman is a global leader in high-accuracy flow measurement, specializing in custody transfer and master meter applications for the oil and gas, petrochemical, and aerospace sectors. With over 90 years of expertise, they offer advanced **Helical Turbine** and **Ultrasonic** flow meters. Their patented helical rotor technology provides unmatched linearity and repeatability, even with varying fluid viscosities, ensuring precise billing and process control. When every drop counts, Faure Herman delivers the reliability and precision your business depends on.

## Product Features
*   **Viscosity Independent:** Specially designed to maintain extreme accuracy even when fluid thickness fluctuates.
*   **Minimal Downtime:** Removable measurement cartridge allows for fast calibration or replacement in the field without removing the meter body.
*   **Advanced Diagnostics:** Multi-path ultrasonic technology provides real-time 3D flow profile monitoring for superior process insight.
*   **Energy Efficient:** FH Series ultrasonic models offer zero pressure drop, significantly reducing overall system energy costs.
*   **Certified Precision:** Fully certified to OIML R117 and API standards for high-stakes commercial custody transfer.
