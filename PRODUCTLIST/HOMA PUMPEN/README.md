# HOMA Submersible Pumps - Engineering Excellence for Wastewater & Sewage

## Short Description
Tackle the toughest pumping challenges with HOMA. Built for municipal and industrial wastewater, HOMA submersible pumps offer non-clogging performance and superior motor protection.

## Full Description
HOMA Pumpen is a world-class manufacturer of submersible pumps, known for their engineering excellence in municipal wastewater treatment, industrial process water, and residential drainage. From compact basement drainage pumps to massive non-clogging sewage pumps, HOMA products are built for the most demanding environments. Their pumps feature advanced motor protection and superior sealing technology to ensure maximum reliability and long service life, even when handling solids-laden, fibrous, or chemically aggressive media.

## Product Features
*   **Clog-Free Operation:** Specially designed single-channel and vortex impellers handle large solids and fibrous matter without blockage.
*   **Dual Protection Sealing:** Tandem silicon-carbide mechanical seals with an oil-filled chamber for ultimate motor protection.
*   **Hazardous Environment Ready:** Available with ATEX and FM Global certifications for hazardous (Class I, Div 1) locations.
*   **Intelligent Monitoring:** Integrated moisture and temperature sensors proactively prevent motor failure by alerting operators to issues.
*   **Rugged Construction:** Choice of heavy-duty Grey Cast Iron or high-grade Stainless Steel for long-term durability in aggressive fluids.
